Rainbow Land ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use Rainbow Land at rainbowland.cc and the desktop application ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Display name, handle, email address, and optional profile details (pronouns, pride flag, bio) when you register.
  • Content You Post: Videos, live streams, messages, and comments published through the Service.
  • Usage Data: Interactions (likes, follows, saves), session timestamps, and app usage analytics.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and identifiers for the desktop application.
  • Third-Party OAuth Tokens: When you connect TikTok, YouTube, or Twitch, we receive OAuth access tokens solely to enable streaming. We never store your passwords for any third-party service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Rainbow Land Service.
  • To enable multi-platform live streaming on your behalf after you explicitly authorize platform connections.
  • To generate AI-powered suggestions via Grok (xAI). No messages are stored by Rainbow Land beyond your active session.
  • To send important service notices (security alerts, account activity).
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Connected streaming platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Twitch) — only as required to fulfill your streaming requests and only with your explicit OAuth authorization.
  • xAI (Grok) — for AI-powered features. See xAI's Privacy Policy.
  • Infrastructure providers — who assist in operating our servers, subject to confidentiality obligations.
  • Law enforcement — when required by applicable law.

4. TikTok Integration

Rainbow Land integrates with TikTok's API to enable streaming and chat. When you authorize this integration:

  • We request the minimum OAuth scopes necessary for live streaming and chat reading.
  • Your TikTok access token is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to Rainbow Land servers.
  • You may revoke TikTok access at any time from TikTok's app settings or within Rainbow Land's Studio settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain account data while your account is active. To request deletion of your account and associated data, email privacy@rainbowland.cc. Third-party OAuth tokens are stored locally on your device only and can be removed at any time.

6. Security

We use TLS/HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and JWT-based authentication with refresh tokens. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext.

7. Children's Privacy

Rainbow Land is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Contact us at privacy@rainbowland.cc if you believe a child has provided personal information and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact privacy@rainbowland.cc to exercise these rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app notice or email. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@rainbowland.cc
